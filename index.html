Take a Hike

The above picture is my favorite place in the White Mountains, Echo Lake.

One of my favorite ways to spend my rare free time is through taking the day to hike with my two Siberian Huskies and my boyfriend Kevin. I made it a goal of mine to hike New Hampshire's forty-eight four-thousand footers within the next few years. This blog will document my experiences on reaching my goal, the note-worthy hikes we've discovered in between, and tips and tricks on finding trailheads, packing, etc. I strongly encourage you to "Take a Hike!".
